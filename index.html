ATELIER NENITA

Atelier Nenita is a craft workshop based on foamy, all projects are made from this material, these dolls are known in Spanish as "Fofuchos".

The teacher Rosa Elena Santander sculpts and molds her own creations based on her imagination or sometimes from existing characters.
Currently there are online courses on these projects, you can find more information on this page.

PHOTO GALLERY

Here you can find some photos of the most recent creations of the artisan Rosa Elena Santander Bermeo, you can see that some of these are based on real people and mythological beings, you will find more like these in social networks and classrooms for sale of the author.

CHALLENGES

Constantly the author makes many challenges with her followers through her facebook page, if you want to participate in any of the challenges you can check our other sections of the page for more contact information, those who get to participate can get certain privileges within the community.
